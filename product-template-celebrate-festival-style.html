Engineered Performance

Built with food service in mind, these Beverage-Air units are carefully designed to optimize your storage capabilities. The space-saving design provides all the benefits of two separate units while utilizing half the floor space. Each unit features sturdy stainless steel construction that will hold up against the demands of a busy commercial kitchen, and the included set of casters makes it simple to relocate these units as needed.

Specialized Refrigerant

The Beverage-Air units in this bundle feature an HC refrigerant (R-290). This hydrocarbon refrigerant is better for the environment than traditional HCFC refrigerants yet still offers reliable cooling performance with excellent energy efficiency. The units maintain precise temperature control for optimal food safety.

Questions & Answers

Q: Can these units be used in a food truck or mobile food operation?
A: Yes! These units are excellent for food trucks and mobile operations. The included casters make them easy to secure and move, and their front-breathing design means they can be placed in tight spaces. Just make sure you have adequate ventilation and a stable 115V power source. The self-closing doors are particularly helpful in mobile environments where the vehicle may be in motion.

Q: What is the warranty coverage on these units?
A: These Beverage-Air units come with a comprehensive 3-year parts and labor warranty on the entire unit and an extended 5-year warranty on the compressor. This gives you peace of mind knowing your investment is protected. Beverage-Air also has an extensive service network across North America for convenient repairs if needed.

Q: How much clearance do I need on each side of the units for proper ventilation?
A: Because these units feature front-breathing compressors, you only need clearance at the front for service access. The units can be placed directly against walls on the sides and back, making them ideal for tight spaces and built-in installations. We recommend at least 6" of clearance at the front for optimal performance and serviceability.

Q: Can I stack these units myself or do I need professional installation?
A: While it's possible to stack them yourself, we strongly recommend professional installation for safety reasons. These are heavy commercial units (approximately 200 lbs each), and proper stacking ensures stability and safe operation. The units come with stacking brackets and detailed instructions.
